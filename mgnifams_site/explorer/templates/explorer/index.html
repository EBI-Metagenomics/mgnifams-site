MGnifams

Starting from MGnify90 cluster representatives (~700M sequences) we kept unannotated ones (400,529,518) and clustered them with linclust from mmseqs into 237,207,063 protein families with 80% identity and 30% coverage. This site presents the 78,494 families that contain at least 100 protein members each.
