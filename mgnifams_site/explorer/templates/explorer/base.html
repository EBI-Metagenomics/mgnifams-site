MGnifams

{% if request.path in "/" %}

Metagenomics derived protein families.

{% endif %}

Starting from MGnify90 cluster representatives (~700M sequences) we sliced pfam annotated parts and kept unannotated sequence with at least 50 amino acids (~548M). We clustered them with linclust (50% identity and 90% coverage) from mmseqs into 465M initial clusters and initiated our protein family generation algorithm. The MGnifams resource currently hosts 99 generated protein families.
